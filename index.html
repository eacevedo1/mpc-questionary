TASK 1: Pitch Discrimination for Isolated Tones

Instructions

You will hear TWO piano tones in each trial.

First, you will hear a REFERENCE tone.
After a short silence, you will hear a COMPARISON tone.

Please decide whether the COMPARISON tone is:

  • SAME as the reference
  • HIGHER than the reference
  • LOWER than the reference

This task establishes your personal pitch-sensitivity baseline.

You will complete 1 PRACTICE trial (with feedback), then 6 test trials.
